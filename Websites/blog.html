  • How to crochet "Chain Stitch"

    • First, create a slip knot and insert your hook in it. Place the yarn over and pull up to create a new loop. With this process, repeat it till you get something that resembles a "chain" like pattern.

  • How to crochet "Single Crochet Stitch"

    • With a new crochet swatch, we will create a slip knot and a beginning chain that will act as a foundation. After that, insert the hook into the next stitch, place the yarn over, and then pull up a loop. Lastly, place the yarn above again and pull through both loops on the hook

  • How to crochet "Double Crochet Stitch"

    • Pull the yarn over and insert the hook onto the next stitch, then put the yarn over and pull up a loop. Do another yarn over and pull through two loops on the hook. Lastly, place the yarn over again and pull through both loops on the hook
